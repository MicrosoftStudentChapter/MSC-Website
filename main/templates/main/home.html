ABOUT US

Microsoft Student Chapter is a technical society which is dedicated to elevate the coding culture of Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology by mentoring and motivating its students to refine their critical thinking, logical reasoning and coding skills. One of the elite societies of TIET.

MSC was formed with the objective of providing students a dynamic platform which feeds their curiousity and develops their technical skills. MSC aims to create a community of versatile enthusiasts with progressive mindset, promoting development holistically which includes injecting soft skills as well. MSC effectuates it by organizing numerous technology driven events including tech meetups and Hackathons and promoting team building. In the long run, MSC plans to cultivate and nurture young minds of TIET to a magnitude where they are proficient enough to compete amongst the nonpareil, thus upheaving the name of TIET.

EVENTS
Microsoft Student Chapter has been the constant flag bearer for providing the youth, a one of its kind platform to showcase the paramount of their technological skills with an effectual vision. The events ranging from Hacktoberfest to Makeathon, require the perfect amalgamation of technical brain racking and impeccable teamwork. Periodic workshops and webinars motivate students to level up their computational skills and showcase their confounding critical thinking skills to challenge their coding apprehension and polish their skills. As of the most esteemed technical societies of TIET, the events conducted by MSC witness massive participation. They foster an environment which promotes teamwork and enhances knowledge in numerous tracks including AI/Machine Learning, Robotics, Block Chain, Cryptography, Algorithms, GraphQL, App development, AR/VR and open hack. With it's events, MSC hopes to tap into the unrealised potential within students as well as provide them an opportunity to discover new fields and probe their mettle.
