IDEATHON 2020
The 21st century belongs to technology. Innovations and new ideas continue to address solutions that shape the future. WORKSHOPS
We, at Microsoft Learn Student Chapter, have always believed in the process of learning together and community guidance. In the spirit of this ideology, we conduct various workshops and AMA sessions within our Open-Source Community throughout the year. These sessions are open to everyone and are hosted by the students themselves who are pursuing their respective fields. We also often invite prominent speakers and instructors to take these workshops.

In the past year, we have conducted workshops on Machine Learning and Deep Learning, Web Development and DevOps, App Development and Cyber Security. A session on Know-Hows of GSoC was also conducted by the Joint Secretary of MLSC, Saurav Shrivastav who himself had cleared GSoC this year.
